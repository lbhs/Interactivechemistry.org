Dimensional Analysis

Learn the methods of Dimensional Analysis through a series of playful scenarios. The initial scene is a "Duel" in which colorful shapes are used to represent units of measurement and the player competes against a bot opponent. Later rounds introduce chemical units of measurement such as grams, moles, liters, and milliliters. Playable on mobile devices!
