Cell Model (2025)

In this fast-paced game, students play as a cell in the human body, trying to acquire reagents needed to produce ATP within mitochondria. Molecules will appear in the bloodstream, and the player must choose which to import (and which to avoid). A second level of the game challenges students to keep a heart cell alive even as each heartbeat uses up ATP.

This game is playable on Laptops, Chromebooks, and Mobile devices!
