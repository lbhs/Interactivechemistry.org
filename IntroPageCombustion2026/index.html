Combustion Reactions (2026)

In this game, you will invest energy to break bonds in fuel molecules such as methane and propane. You will then receive a return on investment as you form new bonds to create the products CO2 and H2O.
In each bond-breaking or bond-forming step, energy changes are made visible using movable Jewels (representing Joules of energy). The concepts of Energy Conservation, Potential Energy, and Activation Energy are clearly demonstrated.

• Combustion Reactions
