Models of Heat Transfer

This simulation demonstrates the transfer of Thermal Energy (Joules) that can occur when molecules collide. Thermal Energy is commonly called Heat. These simulations emphasize concepts of Energy Conservation and Molecular Kinetic Energy.


You can view the source code for this simulation on our Github
