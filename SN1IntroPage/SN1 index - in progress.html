SN1 Reactions

Use an energizable solvent molecule (represented as a jellyfish) to remove a leaving group from a suitable electrophile. Then react the carbonium ion with a nucleophile to produce products. A two-step Energy Profile shows the high energy intermediate characteristic of SN1 reactions. Two versions are offered: one for keyboard controls and one for mobile devices (touchscreen controls)
